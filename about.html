About Us

We are awesome TEAM

Ideal Tech Innovations LLP specializes in precision moulds manufacturing and CNC machining, delivering reliable, high-performance engineering solutions for diverse industrial applications. With a strong focus on accuracy, efficiency, and quality, we transform complex requirements into production-ready solutions.

Equipped with advanced CNC machinery and precision inspection systems, we support projects ranging from single cavity prototypes to high cavity production moulds up to 48 Cavity. Our disciplined processes and technical expertise ensure consistent quality, faster turnaround, and long-term value for our customers


Why Choose Us?

At Ideal Tech Innovations LLP, we combine advanced CNC machining and data-driven design with rigorous in-house testing on our Milacron 200-Ton machine to deliver high-precision, production-ready PET preform and hot runner moulds (up to 48 cavities) that guarantee maximum cycle efficiency and long-term reliability. Through disciplined engineering, advanced CNC technology, and thorough in-house validation, we transform your complex packaging requirements into highly optimized, multi-cavity hot runner moulds that perform flawlessly from the very first shot.

Our Solution

PET Preform Moulds (Up to 48 Cavities)

Highly optimized, balanced hot runner preform moulds designed for standard and wide-mouth neck finishes (such as PCO1881, GME 30.41, 26/22, and Alaska) to maximize production scale.

Custom Jar & Large-Diameter Container Tooling

Specialized multi-cavity hot runner and cold runner moulds for wide-neck jars (up to 96mm and 120mm neck sizes), perfect for food, cosmetic, and bulk packaging.

High-Output Cap & Closure Moulds

Precision-engineered injection moulds for bottle caps and closures, featuring fast-cooling systems and advanced ejection mechanisms to ensure high-speed, defect-free production.
