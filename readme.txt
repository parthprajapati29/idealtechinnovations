## 🛠️ Engineering Capabilities

We support end-to-end tooling projects ranging from single-cavity prototypes to high-cavity production moulds (up to 48 cavities), utilizing advanced hot runner technology.

### 1. Core Tooling Offerings
*   **PET Preform Injection Moulds:** High-performance multi-cavity moulds.
*   **Hot Runner Systems:** Balanced valve-gated and open-gate systems for stringing-free production.
*   **ISBM & EBM Tooling:** Specialized blow-mould designs.
*   **Hot Plate Moulds & Caps/Closures:** Precise tooling for caps (up to 120mm neck sizes).

### 2. Supported Neck Finishes
We design and manufacture moulds compatible with all major global packaging standards:
`PCO1881` • `GME 30.41` • `26/22` • `ALASKA` • `29/21 CTC` • `ROPP` • `Custom Designs`

## 📐 Design & Quality Commitment

Our **"Right First Time"** philosophy is backed by structured, data-driven workflows across our core departments:

*   **Data-Driven Design:** We perform flow simulations, stress analysis, and cooling layouts to optimize cycle times and reduce preform weight.
*   **Meticulous Assembly:** Expert technicians manually align and assemble each component to ensure flawless operation and tool longevity.
*   **Scientific Quality Assurance:** Every single mould is strictly verified using Mitutoyo CMMs and field-tested on our in-house Milacron 200-Ton machine before dispatch.
